Contribute

We are looking for blogs, opinions and views on politics, foreign policy, international trade, the economy, music, theatre, public health, infrastructure, sports, books, law, arts, entertainment etc. Word count - between 700-1200. Original Content: We ask that posts submitted be unique and original. We do not accept posts that have already been published or submitted elsewhere online. But once we accept the article, we may give you permission to publish it subsequently elsewhere and on your personal blog. Acceptance: At present, compensation is a byline, a brief bio (2 sentences) and links to your website, Facebook and Twitter accounts which we will promote if your piece is accepted. If your contribute regularly, we will feature you alongwith our regular writers. In that case we request your photo, but this is optional. Our ground rules are the following- We do not accept any content from anonymous contributors. We are looking for strong opinions which are well argued and concise. We will encourage debate and discussion and we will systematically turn down peices that slander. All facts should be independently verifiable by us. If you would like to report on a news event happening in your locality or anywhere else in the world, send us credible information. We will put our reporters on the job and compile the report. Would you like to point out a story that we missed? Is there a buzz on social media that we overlooked? Tweet, message on FB or write in to us. If we decide to publish that story, your will be acknowledged. By submitting, you acknowledge that you accept our terms, and any form of editing that might occur on behalf of the editorial team.
